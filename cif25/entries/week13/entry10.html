The Avengers (2012)

The Avengers is the 6th movie in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and was released in 2012. It features Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye coming together to stop Loki and the Chitauri invasion.

I love this movie because it brings together all my favorite heroes with amazing chemistry. The action sequences are epic, the humor lands perfectly, and it sets the stage for the MCU as we know it today.
