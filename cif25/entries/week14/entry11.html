Blade (1998)

Blade is a superhero horror film starring Wesley Snipes as the half-vampire, half-human vampire hunter. The film follows Blade as he protects humans from vampires and battles the vampire leader Deacon Frost.

I love this movie because of its dark, stylish tone and Wesley Snipes’ performance. The fight choreography is tight, the visual style is sleek, and the film balances horror and superhero elements brilliantly. Blade stands out in the 90s superhero era for its edgy approach and incredible action sequences.
