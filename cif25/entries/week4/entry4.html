Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019) is a thrilling addition to the Marvel Cinematic Universe, directed by Jon Watts. The film follows Peter Parker (Tom Holland) as he embarks on a European school trip, only to be thrust into action when Nick Fury (Samuel L. Jackson) recruits him to help combat mysterious elemental creatures wreaking havoc across the continent.

Why I Love This Movie

I love this film because it reminds me of a simler time in my life. I remeber sitting in the theater on an early saturday morning with my dad and being so excited to finally see the sequel Spider-Man movie. I had seen homecoming in theaters and loved it so much that I was excited to see the next one. The movie was so much fun and had a great story. I loved the chemistry between Tom Holland and Zendaya. The action scenes were also really well done and the special effects were impressive. Overall, it was a great movie and I would highly recommend it to anyone. Watching it again brought back a lot of good memories and made me appreciate the movie even more.
