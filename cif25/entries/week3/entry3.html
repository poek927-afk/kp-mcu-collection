Iron Man is a 2008 superhero film directed by Jon Favreau and based on the Marvel Comics character Tony Stark, a genius billionaire and industrialist who becomes the armored superhero Iron Man. The film stars Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark, with Gwyneth Paltrow, Terrence Howard, and Jeff Bridges.

The story follows Stark as he is captured by terrorists and forced to build a weapon, but instead creates a suit of powered armor to escape. Upon returning home, he refines the technology and vows to use it to protect the world, setting the stage for the entire Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Why I like this movie

I like this movie because it redefined superhero films for a new generation — with sharp humor, brilliant tech visuals, and the perfect performance from Robert Downey Jr. It’s one of those movies that made being a hero look cool, smart, and deeply human.

Watching Iron Man now still feels powerful — it’s not just the start of the MCU, but the foundation of modern blockbuster storytelling.
